## Discover users
Here you can discover new capybaras ,  in this page you can draw random capybara according to your gender. Card contains information about your potential friend like interests, age, name, description  and profile image

![[Pasted image 20220612112337.png]]

If you want to "Like" that capybara and you are looking for contact from him/her
select action "WOW". Then that user will be added to your <b>Matches</b> (when potential partner also liked you) or <b>Likes</b> when this capybara had not react to you yet. 

![[Pasted image 20220612113145.png]]

if you want to search futher, press "MEH", then page reloads with other capybara.

![[Pasted image 20220612113138.png]]

If you liked all capybaras in app database, proper information card will be showed.
